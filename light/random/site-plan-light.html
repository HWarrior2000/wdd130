Light Restoration Site Plan

Helaman Tillotson

WDD 130-2

Overview

Purpose

To help the business reach more people. To show that restoration is cheaper and can provide a better final product that purchasing replacement headlights. Show examples of what a restored headlight can look like.

Audience

People looking to make their car look better. This can be people selling a car, or wanting to fix theirs up. People looking to increase the brightness of their headlights. The site will most likely be opened from a desktop or laptop.
